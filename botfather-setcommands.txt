# Instructions:

1 - Talk to the botfather
2 - Use: /setcommands
3 - Select your tip bot
4 - Paste this:

help - Read the help.
balance - Check your balance. 
tip - Reply to a message to tip. Example: /tip 50
send - Send funds to a Telegram user. Example: /send 100 @LightningTipBot
invoice - Create a Lightning invoice to receive payments. Example: /invoice 1000
pay - Pay a Lightning invoice. Example: /pay lnbc10n1ps...
